Rules For Submissions

What We Accept

  • Poetry (Sonnets, Ballads, Free Verse) - no longer than 1500 words, up to 5 pieces
  • Prose (Short Stories, Essays, Fiction/Non-Fiction) - no longer than 3500 words, up to 2 pieces

Please ensure that all pieces are written in Times New Roman font, along with standard formatting to ensure legibility. Exceptions can be made per request.

All writers must include a third person written biography when submitting their works.

The Typewriter will not accept pieces that have already been published.

The Typewriter will not accept any pieces containing bigotry of any kind. Though the art of sharing ones stories is addicting, we do not condone any creations made to belittle/mock marginalized groups of people .

What We Are Looking For

We want writers to create pieces pretraining to the given theme in any way they interpret them. There is no wrong or right way to go about writing a piece for our magazine. We want the pieces that make you smile at your screen, the ones you're eager to share, the ones that make your fingers dance across your keyboard, and the ones that remind you why you love to write.
